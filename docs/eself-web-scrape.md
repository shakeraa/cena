## 3. Technology & Product

### Core Platform
- **No-code avatar creation studio** -- businesses and educators can customize avatar appearance, personality, knowledge base, and capabilities without technical expertise
- **Photorealistic AI avatars** -- customizable appearance with accurate lip-syncing across languages
- **LLM-agnostic** -- connect any large language model or custom API
- **Response time:** Under 1 second (PR states sub-1s); Product Hunt states under 1.7 seconds (vs. OpenAI's 2.4s)
- **30+ languages** supported with native-quality lip sync
- **Real-time visual engine** -- generates images, charts, videos, and product demos on-the-fly during conversation
- **Multimodal input** -- visual comprehension capabilities (can understand what user shows on camera)

### Product Lines (from eself.ai pages, now redirected)
- **AI Video Chat** -- real-time video chatbot for customer engagement
- **AI Tutor** -- personalized education with adaptive learning
- **Conversational AI** -- face-to-face interaction bots
- **Digital Twins** -- hyper-realistic AI replicas of individuals

### Integrations
- Salesforce, Calendly, YouTube
- CRM and LMS systems
- Deployment: web, mobile, kiosk

### Use Cases
- **Education:** AI tutors that adapt to individual student strengths, weaknesses, and language preferences
- **Financial Services:** AGI Bank (Brazil, 10M+ customers) used eSelf agents
- **Real Estate:** Christie's Portugal deployed eSelf virtual agents
- **Financial Advisory:** DL Holdings (Hong Kong)
- **Enterprise Sales & Support:** 24/7 automated conversations

### Traction (as of Dec 2024 seed announcement)
- "Millions of real-time conversations" powered
- "Hundreds of thousands of consumer users" generated organically from website demo

## 6. CET Partnership & National AI Tutoring Pilot

### Overview
eSelf partnered with Israel's **Center for Educational Technology (CET)** to lead the **world's first countrywide rollout of AI tutoring**, with **Harvard University** as academic advisor.

### Announcement Date
April 22, 2025

### Key Details
- **Pilot scope:** Begins with **10,000 students** in A/B testing (launched May 2025)
- **Initial subject:** Hebrew language instruction
- **Planned expansion:** All K-12 subjects in Israel
- **Academic advisor:** Harvard University -- Victor Pereira, Faculty and Co-Chair of Teaching and Teaching Leadership program, guiding methodology and impact evaluation
- CET is Israel's **largest K-12 textbook publisher**

### Technology Deployed
- Interactive AI avatar serving as face-to-face virtual learning companion
- Real-time spoken interaction in Hebrew
- On-the-fly generation of images and videos to accompany lessons
- Adaptive learning that personalizes to individual student strengths and challenges
- Tailored to classroom content

### Educational Rationale
- **The Two Sigma Problem** (Benjamin Bloom, 1984): one-on-one tutored students perform **two standard deviations** better than classroom peers
- UNESCO projects a deficit of **44 million** primary and secondary educators globally
- Student test scores at historic lows post-pandemic
- U.S. Department of Education identifies AI tutoring as a promising solution for educational inequality

### Key Quotes
- **Tzachi Langer** (VP Marketing & Product, CET): The partnership "marks a significant global milestone in leveraging artificial intelligence for the education sector."
- **Victor Pereira** (Harvard): "Precisely how AI should be integrated into education: not as a replacement for human educators, but as a powerful supplement."
- **Dr. Alan Bekker** (CEO, eSelf): Called eSelf "the first AI tutoring initiative to be deployed at a national scale."

### Philosophy
"Designed not to replace teachers, but to extend their reach -- helping students learn independently and build academic confidence."

## 12. Key Takeaways for Cena

1. **eSelf is no longer independent.** Acquired by Kaltura (Nov 2025, $27M). The CET partnership and education pilot now operate under Kaltura's umbrella. Any integration or partnership discussions must go through Kaltura.

2. **The CET pilot is real and significant.** 10,000 students in A/B testing starting May 2025, with Hebrew language instruction. Harvard is the academic advisor. This is the world's first nationwide AI tutoring rollout.

3. **Technology is proven at scale.** Millions of real-time conversations, sub-1-second response times, 30+ languages, no-code studio, LLM-agnostic. Enterprise clients included AGI Bank (Brazil, 10M+ customers), Christie's Portugal, and DL Holdings.

4. **The team has deep pedigree.** Alan Bekker built Snap's My AI chatbot (Voca.ai exit for $70-100M). CTO from Unit 8200 with NLP master's from Technion. Both are now at Kaltura.

5. **No open-source code exists.** Entirely proprietary. No GitHub repos, no published technical papers from the company, no public APIs documented.

6. **The education vertical was a key differentiator.** eSelf positioned AI tutoring (adaptive, face-to-face, multilingual) as its flagship education use case, grounded in the Two Sigma Problem research.

7. **Pilot results are not yet public.** As of this scrape date (March 2026), no published outcomes from the CET pilot have been found. The pilot launched ~10 months ago.

8. **Competitive landscape note.** eSelf was acquired partly because Kaltura wanted avatar/conversational AI for its learning platform. This validates the market but also removes eSelf as a standalone competitor/partner option.
