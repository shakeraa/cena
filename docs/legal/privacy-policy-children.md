# Cena Children's Privacy Notice

**Status: DRAFT — awaiting Cena legal counsel review**
**Last updated: 2026-04-11**
**Reading level: Flesch-Kincaid grade 5 or below**

## 1. What is this page?

This page tells you how Cena uses your info. Cena is a learning app. We keep your info safe. Your parent or guardian said it was OK for you to use Cena.

## 2. What info do we keep?

When you use Cena, we keep a small amount of info about you:

- **Your name** — the name you pick when you sign up.
- **Your email** — so you can log in.
- **Your age** — so we know the right rules to follow.
- **Your answers** — the work you do in learning sessions.
- **Your chats** — what you say to the AI tutor.

## 3. Why do we keep it?

We use your info to help you learn. Cena picks questions that are right for you. It tracks how well you are doing so you and your teacher can see your progress.

We do NOT sell your info. We do NOT share it with other kids. We do NOT use it to show you ads.

## 4. No ads, no tracking

Cena does not show you any ads. We do not track what you do outside of Cena. We do not let other companies track you inside Cena.

## 5. Your parent gave permission

Before you could use Cena, your parent or guardian gave us permission. They told us it was OK to keep your info.

If your parent changes their mind, they can ask us to delete all of your info. You can ask them to do this at any time.

## 6. What can your parent do?

Your parent or guardian can:

- **See your info** — ask us to show them what we have.
- **Delete your info** — ask us to erase everything about you.
- **Stop us from keeping your info** — which means your account will close.

To do any of these, your parent can email us at privacy@cena.edu.

## 7. How we keep your info safe

We lock your info with strong security, like a safe. Only the people who need to see it can see it. We check our security often to make sure it stays strong.

## 8. How long we keep your info

We keep your info while you have an account. If your parent asks us to delete it, we do so within 30 days.

Your answers and chats are kept for up to 12 months, then we erase them.

## 9. Questions?

If you or your parent have any questions, email us at privacy@cena.edu. We will write back within 30 days.
