# Regional & Emerging Ed-Tech Competitors Intelligence Report
## Competitive Analysis for CENA Learning Platform

**Report Date:** March 2026  
**Analyst:** Competitive Intelligence Team  
**Scope:** Regional and emerging ed-tech competitors in Turkish, MENA, and Israeli markets

---

## Executive Summary

This report analyzes regional and emerging ed-tech competitors that pose potential threats or offer learning opportunities for CENA. Key findings:

1. **erkanhoca_mat** - YouTube-based Turkish math educator (limited app presence)
2. **Coursera Mobile** - Global leader with strong gamification (4.8★ iOS, 4.2★ Android)
3. **edX Mobile** - University-focused with offline capabilities (4.7★ both platforms)
4. **NEW ENTRANTS DISCOVERED:**
   - **Abwaab** (Jordan) - $27.5M funded, 12M downloads, strong MENA presence
   - **Noon Academy** (Saudi Arabia) - $21M funded, 16M+ learners, social learning pioneer
   - **Doping Hafıza** (Turkey) - Turkey's largest AI-powered education platform
   - **Loora** (Israel) - $21.25M funded, AI English tutor
   - **Al-Mentor** (UAE) - $14.5M funded, Arabic video learning platform
