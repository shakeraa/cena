### CENA's Competitive Position

**CENA's Unique Advantages (No competitor has all):**
1. **FSRS Spaced Repetition** - Only Anki matches; no commercial competitor uses FSRS
2. **Knowledge Graph with Prerequisites** - Unique in the market
3. **Socratic AI Tutoring Method** - Different from direct-instruction competitors
4. **Quality-Gated Streaks** - Superior to login-based streaks
5. **Arabic + Hebrew RTL Support** - Unique dual-RTL capability
6. **Wellbeing Features** (time limits, bedtime mode) - Largely unique
7. **Deep Study Mode** - Differentiated from microlearning competitors

### Strategic Imperatives

1. **Defend RTL Leadership** - CENA is the ONLY platform with full Arabic + Hebrew RTL support
2. **Leverage FSRS Differentiation** - Only CENA and Anki use state-of-the-art spaced repetition
3. **Close Camera/OCR Gap** - HIGH priority feature missing from CENA's offering
4. **Enhance Gamification** - Learn from Duolingo's industry-leading engagement mechanics
5. **Build AI Tutor Personality** - Synthesis shows the power of warm, encouraging AI tone
