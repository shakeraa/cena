# Competitive Intelligence Report: AI-Powered Tutoring & Problem-Solving Tools

**Research Date:** January 2025  
**Analyst:** Competitive Intelligence Team  
**Competitors Analyzed:** Photomath, Mathway, Wolfram Alpha, Socratic by Google, Numerade

---

## Executive Summary

This report provides a comprehensive competitive analysis of five leading AI-powered tutoring and problem-solving tools. The analysis covers 13 feature categories with special focus on AI capabilities, camera/OCR functionality, step-by-step solution approaches, and monetization strategies.

### Key Findings:
- **Photomath** (Google-owned) leads in camera OCR accuracy and step-by-step clarity with 220M+ downloads
- **Mathway** (Chegg-owned) offers broad subject coverage but struggles with user satisfaction (mixed reviews)
- **Wolfram Alpha** provides the most advanced computational engine but has a dated interface
- **Socratic by Google** was discontinued as a standalone app in October 2024, now integrated into Google Lens
- **Numerade** differentiates with video-based solutions and AI tutor "Ace" powered by ChatGPT-4
