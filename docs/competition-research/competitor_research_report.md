# Education Giants & Gamification Benchmarks - Competitive Intelligence Report

## Executive Summary

This report provides comprehensive competitive analysis of four major education platforms: **Khan Academy**, **Khanmigo**, **Duolingo**, and **Kahoot!**. Special focus is given to Duolingo as the gold standard for gamification mechanics in educational technology.
