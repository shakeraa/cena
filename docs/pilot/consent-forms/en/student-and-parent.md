## Dear parent, dear student,

We invite you to take part in an educational pilot we are running at
the **Cena** platform in partnership with your school. The goal is to
test how well a platform interface that leads with Arabic supports
Arabic-speaking students learning mathematics. Participation is
**100% voluntary** and will not affect the student's school grades or
the family's relationship with the school.

## What is the pilot?

- Runs for **12 weeks**.
- The student receives a Cena account with a fully-Arabic interface.
- The student works on mathematics problems matched to their level.
- We collect only the student's answers and whether they felt
  comfortable — no additional personal data.

## What do we ask of the family / student?

1. **A short pre-pilot assessment** (45 min) to measure starting level.
2. **Use Cena** for weekly study sessions over 12 weeks.
3. **A short post-pilot assessment** (45 min) to measure change.
4. **One short question after each session** about how the session
   felt.

## What will NOT happen

- The student's identity **will not** appear in any report or paper.
- The student's data **will not** be shared with anyone outside the
  pilot team.
- The data **will not** be used to train AI models.
- The student's relationship with the school **will not** be affected
  — all academic rights remain intact.

## Participant rights

- **Withdraw** at any time, for any reason, with no negative effect.
- **Inspect** what we have collected and **request deletion** within
  30 days.
- **Contact** us at any time: **privacy@cena.edu**
  (counsel to confirm this mailbox is active).

## Psychological safety

- We monitor weekly how comfortable the student felt, using the
  one-question session check.
- If unusual distress appears, **the pilot pauses immediately** for
  that student and resumes only after review by a qualified
  educational team.
- The student's academic support from school continues uninterrupted
  whether the pilot resumes or not.

## Retention

- Individual data: **12 months** after pilot end, then deleted.
- Aggregate data (general statistics, no names): may be retained for
  future research.
