The graph above shows the function $f(x) = x^2 - 4x + 3$. Find the coordinates of the vertex and determine the range of the function.
Worked Example
For $g(x) = x^2 - 2x - 3$, we complete the square: $g(x) = (x - 1)^2 - 4$. The vertex is $(1, -4)$ and the range is $[-4, \infty)$.

B Vertex $(2, 1)$, range $[1, \infty)$
